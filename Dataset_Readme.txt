I created the dataset used in this project by accessing population data from a few sources.

Tee majority of the data came from census data in this .pdf file:
http://www.census.gov/population/www/censusdata/PopulationofStatesandCountiesoftheUnitedStates1790-1990.pdf

For data from 1990 until 2015 I used a combination of searches at 

population.us and wikipedia.com.

I created a spreadsheet in google charts and used it for formatting and cleaning of the data.
